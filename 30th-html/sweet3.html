BADHUSHA

 
        1.5cup	    Maida / All Purpose flour
        1/2 tsp     	Baking Powder
        2 tbsp	     Yogurt / Curd
        1/4 cup	    Ghee / Clarified Butter
        1/2 tsp	    Sugar
        3 -4 tbsp	  Milk

           
For Sugar Syrup

        1 Cup	      Sugar
        1/2          Cup	Water
        2	         Cardamom
        10	         Saffron
       few drops 	lemon juice

Instructions:

 Mix maida, yogurt, ghee, baking powder, milk and make a rough dough
        let it rest for 10 -15 minutes
        Make Sugar Syrup and flavor
        make small balls and fry the badusha over medium heat
        dunk it into the sugar syrup for 3 - 4 minutes
        Our Delicious Badusha is ready to be enjoyed
