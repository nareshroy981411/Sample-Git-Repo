LADDU


INGREDIENTS

  • 1/2 tinNestlé MILKMAID
  • 200 gmNestlé Everyday Shahi Ghee
  • 2/3 cup (100 ml)Refined oil
  • 500 gm, coarseBesan
  • 20, choppedCashew Nuts
  • 25, choppedAlmonds

    • Take heavy bottom kadai, add ghee, oil and besan. Cook on medium flame for 10 to 12 minutes or till besan turns golden brown. Add chopped nuts, mix well and keep aside to cool down. Once cool add the Nestlé MILKMAID and mix well, keep aside for 15 minutes. Divide the mixture into 24 portions and gently roll into laddus.
